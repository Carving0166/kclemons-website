Common questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know before you book.

Do I need a truck to tow the trailers?

It depends on your load. The 5×8 dump trailer requires a 2⁵⁄₁₆″ hitch ball. The 16’ Rogers Welding utility trailer has a 7,000 lb GVWR with a 2″ ball coupler — any vehicle capable of towing the weight of your load can handle it. You are responsible for ensuring your tow vehicle is rated for your intended load. When in doubt, give us a call and we’ll help you figure it out.

How does the damage deposit work?

A $150 deposit is collected at booking. It’s returned within 3–5 business days after the trailer comes back clean, undamaged, and on time. We document everything with photos at pickup and return.

Are there mileage fees?

None. You pay the flat daily rate and that’s it. Drive it as far as you need — your bill stays the same.

Can I rent for multiple days?

Yes. Multi-day and weekly rates are available. Contact us before booking and we’ll set up the right rate for your project timeline.

Where do I pick up and return?

Warsaw, Indiana. The exact address is provided with your booking confirmation. We’re locally owned — you’re picking up from a real neighbor.

What if I return it late?

Late returns are charged at an hourly rate agreed at pickup. If you think you’ll run long, just text us — we’ll work with you as long as the trailer isn’t already reserved.
